Introducing scrollit.js

scrollit.js is a very simple jquery script that smoothly scrolls to a target. It increases user experience and makes your website a little bit nicer. Beside jquey you additionaly need jquery-ui for easing effects. scrollit.js is easy to use and works allmost out of the box. See the howto for details.

How to integrate scrollit.js

This example is how I integrated it on this page. You may check the source-code, too.
First identify your targets ... 


...        
<div class="row" id="credits">...</div>
<div class="row" id="credits">...</div>
<div class="row" id="credits">...</div>
...

... and then add href and onclick to the corresponding links. 


...        
<ul class="right">
  <li class="active"><a href="#intro" onclick="scrollItWrapper('#intro'); return false;">About</a></li>
  <li class="active"><a href="#howto" onclick="scrollItWrapper('#howto'); return false;">How to use</a></li>
  <li class="active"><a href="#credits" onclick="scrollItWrapper('#credits'); return false;">Credits</a></li>
</ul>
...

Finally load scrollit.js just before the body ends ... 


...
<script src="http://code.jquery.com/ui/1.10.3/jquery-ui.js"></script>
<script src="js/foundation.min.js"></script>
<script src="js/scrollit.js"></script>
<script>
  $(document).foundation();
</script>
</body>
...

... and that's it with your html-file!

Don't forget to put scrollit.js into your js-directory.
